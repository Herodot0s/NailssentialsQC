# Sketch Manifest

## Design Direction
Replace button-based time in/out with an iPhone lockscreen-style slider interaction. The goal is to make check-in/out feel more tactile and intentional (preventing accidental taps). The design should feel premium and align with the NailssentialsQC brand (Terracotta #B8794E, minimal/Swiss aesthetic with `rounded-none` corners).

Reference: Old iPhone "Slide to Unlock" interaction (single horizontal swipe). Frappe HRMS provides the structural context (employee checkin records, timestamp tracking, shift management).

## Reference Points
- iPhone "Slide to Unlock" (2007-2016) — horizontal swipe with spotlight text
- Frappe HRMS Employee Checkin Panel — timestamp display, quick actions
- NailssentialsQC brand: Terracotta #B8794E, `rounded-none`, uppercase labels, font-serif headings
