# Summary: Colorize Manage Services

The `ManageServices.tsx` page has been strategically colorized to enhance wayfinding and brand presence while maintaining management-level restraint.

## Key Accomplishments

### 1. Dynamic Category Wayfinding
- **OKLCH Color Mapping**: Implemented a harmonious color system using `OKLCH` to assign unique hues to each service category (Nails, Spa, Hair, etc.).
- **Visual Wayfinding**: Category chips and badges now use these persistent hues, making it easier for managers to visually scan the service list by category.

### 2. Premium Brand Accents
- **The "Kiln" CTA**: Upgraded the "New Service" button with a sophisticated `Kiln Terracotta` linear gradient and a premium shadow.
- **Vibrant Badges**: The "Popular" badge now uses a solid, saturated brand fill with a subtle shadow to stand out without feeling "loud".
- **Color-Coded Prices**: Prices are now rendered in bold `Kiln Terracotta` to emphasize the financial aspect of service management.

### 3. Semantic Clarity
- **Glow-State Indicators**: Active and Disabled states now use Kiln-palette semantic colors (`Forest Confirm`, `Brick Error`) with soft glow effects, providing immediate visual feedback on service status.
- **Richer Neutrals**: All table rows and secondary elements now use brand-tinted neutrals (`Bisque Wash`, `Linen Mist`) to avoid cold, clinical grays.

## Visual Verification
The interface now feels vibrant yet professional. The use of OKLCH ensures that even though multiple hues are present, they maintain a consistent perceived lightness and brand harmony.
