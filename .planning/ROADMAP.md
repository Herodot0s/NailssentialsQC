# Roadmap

## Phases

- [ ] **Phase 1: Premium UI Foundations** - Establish global design tokens and transition animations
- [ ] **Phase 2: Customer Experience Overhaul** - Redesign the customer landing page and booking flow
- [ ] **Phase 3: Admin Experience Overhaul** - Modernize the manager and staff dashboards
- [ ] **Phase 4: Headless CMS & Nail Art Gallery** - Enable dynamic website content management and public nail art exhibits
- [ ] **Phase 5: Sequential Service Packages** - Allow creation and booking of service bundles with correct commission logic
- [ ] **Phase 6: Manager Analytics Dashboard** - Provide interactive revenue, staff, and retention reports

## Phase Details

**Goal**: Global design tokens and transition animations are established across the platform

**Success Criteria** (what must be TRUE):
