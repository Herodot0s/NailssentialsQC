# Research Paper: NailssentialsQC - Premium Salon Management System

## 1. Project Context & Background
NailssentialsQC is a specialized salon management platform designed to streamline operations for nail salons. Built using a modern full-stack architecture (React 19, Express.js, Prisma ORM, and PostgreSQL), the system caters to three distinct user archetypes: Customers, Staff, and Managers. The project originated as a "brownfield" application that required significant hardening, including bug fixes, security enhancements, and a complete UI/UX overhaul to meet premium boutique standards.

## 2. Rationale
The nail salon industry often relies on fragmented or outdated booking systems that fail to provide a seamless, high-end experience for both clients and service providers. NailssentialsQC addresses this by consolidating booking, staff attendance, payroll, and business analytics into a single, unified platform. By adopting a "Premium Experience" philosophy, the system ensures that the digital touchpoint of the business matches the high-quality physical services provided in the salon, thereby increasing customer retention and operational efficiency.

## 3. Objective of the Study
### General Objective
To develop and implement a robust, production-ready salon management system that provides a premium user experience while ensuring data integrity, security, and operational transparency.

### Specific Objectives
- **UI/UX Excellence**: To implement a design system inspired by premium industry standards (e.g., Airbnb) to enhance user engagement.
- **Operational Efficiency**: To automate complex business logic such as commission calculations, payroll generation, and staff attendance tracking.
- **Data-Driven Insights**: To provide managers with advanced analytics regarding revenue trends, staff performance, and customer retention.
- **Security & Reliability**: To achieve high test coverage (80-90%) and implement industry-standard security protocols (JWT, role-based access control, rate limiting).

## 4. Scope & Delimitation
### Scope
- **User Roles**: Comprehensive workflows for Customers (booking, profiles), Staff (clock-in/out, commissions), and Managers (full system administration).
- **Core Modules**: Appointment management, Service Catalog, Marketing CMS, Advanced Analytics, and Payroll.
- **Technology**: Deployment-ready architecture on Vercel with Dockerized local development.

### Delimitation
- **Payments**: The system currently manages booking and service tracking but defers actual payment processing to offline/in-salon methods.
- **Notifications**: Limited to Email and In-App notifications; SMS integration is outside the current scope.
- **Platform**: Web-based responsive application; native mobile applications (iOS/Android) are not included.
- **Tenancy**: Designed for a single-salon deployment; multi-tenant/multi-location support is excluded.
