SET OF GOALS FOR CUSTOMERS
Easy Booking of Appointment - Let users quickly browse, book, reschedule, or cancel appointments for services 
Service Selection - Show services, prices, duration, and allow stylist selection, showing what’s popular, recommendation
Mode of Payments - Support multiple payment methods with safe transactions, automatically giving receipts to all transactions
User Accounts - Store profiles, booking history, and preferences
Notifications - Send confirmations, reminders, and updates (including appointment reminders)
Reviews & Feedback - Allow ratings and reviews for services and salons
User-Friendly Design – Simple interface, works on most devices 

SET OF GOALS FOR STAFFS
Booking Management - approve, reschedule, cancel appointments for clearer transactions 
Customer Information Management - View customer details and booking history
Service Management - Update and manage services (price, duration, availability)
Customer Communication  - Communicate with customers (notifications, updates, messages)
Payment Verification - Verify payments and booking status
Feedback Management - Handle customer feedback and reviews 
User-Friendly Design – Simple interface, works on most devices
