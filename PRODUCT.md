# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Salon managers, staff (nail technicians), and customers. Managers and staff use the system in a fast-paced retail environment (often on tablets or phones) to manage operations. Customers use it on mobile and desktop to book appointments easily.

## Product Purpose
A comprehensive salon management system for NailssentialsQC. It handles online booking, staff attendance, commission tracking, and analytics, ensuring smooth operations and a seamless customer experience.

## Brand Personality
Professional, Modern, Minimalist. The interface should feel as trustworthy and cleanly structured as Airbnb, prioritizing clarity, space, and ease of use.

## Anti-references
- Cluttered, dense "enterprise" dashboards with too much data crammed into one screen.
- Stereotypical, hyper-feminine "salon" aesthetics (excessive pinks, cursive script fonts).
- Generic SaaS "card-in-a-gray-background" templates.

## Design Principles
- **Spatial clarity:** Use generous whitespace and clear typography to group information, relying on layout rather than borders or boxes.
- **Progressive disclosure:** Show users only what they need to see for their current task.
- **Obvious affordances:** Primary actions should be immediately clear, especially for fast-paced salon staff.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
WCAG AA compliance. High contrast for readability in varying lighting conditions (e.g., bright salon environments). Large touch targets for mobile and tablet use.
