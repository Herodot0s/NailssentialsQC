# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Customers booking salon services, staff managing their schedules and commissions, and managers overseeing daily operations and analytics.

## Product Purpose
A reliable, bug-free, and premium salon management system that users can trust for daily operations, featuring online booking, staff attendance tracking, commission management, and detailed business analytics.

## Brand Personality
Luxury, tranquility, rich. A private retreat where every visual detail feels handmade, conveying warmth without saccharine, luxury without ostentation, and craft without pretension.

## Anti-references
Clinical SaaS aesthetics, generic AI-generated spa templates, glassmorphism as decoration, identical card grids with icon-title-text patterns, and gradient text effects.

## Design Principles
- **Handmade Craft:** Visual details should feel meticulously crafted and intentional, avoiding the feel of generic templates.
- **Warm Restraint:** Create an inviting, tranquil atmosphere using rich, warm tones rather than cold, clinical defaults.
- **Purposeful Motion:** Motion should be responsive and tactile, acknowledging interaction to build trust without performing unnecessarily.
- **Editorial Elegance:** Typography should bring editorial gravity to headings without being ornate, maintaining clear hierarchy and readability.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
Targeting WCAG AA compliance. Ensure sufficient color contrast and legible text scaling across all interfaces.
